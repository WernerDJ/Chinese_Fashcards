The text for this video was generated by DeepSeek after feeding it with the 400 Chinese words that I had learnt so far and asking it to generate a text using those words, the text emotionally charged. It resulted in a rather sad text but good enough. I generated the audio with an OpenAI based agent, that read the text while I recorded it. Then the audio was fed into Kdenlive to an audio channel, the images from internet added to the video channel and the subtitles were made with Aegisub
The text was translated into Spanish by DeepSeek with the instruction to follow the word order of the Chinese grammar, independently on how wrong it will look in Spanish. This is for me to associate the Chinese words, as they are spoken, with their meaning.
